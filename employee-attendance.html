Punctuality

Employees are expected to check in by 10:00 AM. Check-ins after this time will automatically be recorded as an absence. To be marked present after this cutoff, you must inform management of your reason for the delay.

System Usage

Attendance should be recorded only through the official check-in/check-out system. Any attempt to manipulate or falsify attendance records will be treated seriously and may lead to termination.
